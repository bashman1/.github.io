Welcome to Two Hands One Life

Two Hands One Life (THOL) have been in existence since 2016 supporting the poor needy children and youths, orphans, abandoned, and vulnerable including children with disabilities, helping them to fulfill their rights in Uganda. The organization was conceived by a group of individuals who wished to give a hand to vulnerable groups especially by helping the most marginalized vulnerable by caring those groups of people living in dire need for help.

 

“There is no exercise better for the heart than reaching down and lifting people up.” “When we give cheerfully and accept gratefully, everyone is blessed.”

Our Context

57% of Uganda’s population is below the age of 18. According to MoGLSD, 94% of Uganda’s children are at risk, and the Government is only able to help 11% of these children, leaving 89% of marginalized children without Government support. This makes the interventions of NGOs of paramount importance.
